\section*{Problem 2 (14 points)}

Consider the following data matrix

$$
    D = \begin{matrix}
            & X_1   & X_2   & X_3 \\
        x_1 &  0.3  & 23    & 5.6 \\
        x_2 &  0.4  &  1    & 5.2 \\
        x_3 &  1.8  &  4    & 5.2 \\
        x_4 &  6.0  & 50    & 5.1 \\
        x_5 & -0.5  & 34    & 5.7 \\
        x_6 &  0.4  & 19    & 5.4 \\
        x_7 &  1.1  & 11    & 5.5 \\
    \end{matrix}
$$

    \newline
